The Genesis

You will quickly learn that, in this blog, we love a good story, and the creation of /Serendipitea/ is no exception. It begins with two friends, one tea pot, and an unquenchable curisitea. On a sunny Sunday, they stumbled into a conversation about life, hope and resilience, that resounded deeply. So, cup after cup, they uncovered the secret that exists in everything: the serendipitea of life, the encounters and happenings so casual and unexpected, yet sometimes life-changing...

What to expect

Look out for a freshly brewed article on the first day of the month, written by yours truly, on topics ranging from literature, food, and societea. Over here, everything is completely artisanal, (including the website!) so please reach out to us on Instagram for any queries or suggestions.
