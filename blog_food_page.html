For the foodies

You only need to know one thing about us, and one thing only: we <3 sugar. So naturally, we have curated for you the unmissable culinary finds we have come across around the world, because sharing is caring (and we want to get discounts haha just kidding) on the rare occasion that we don't just eat desert for the whole meal.

#1 Farro Bakery

Bristol, United Kingdom

Oh my this place is famous! Caution if you want to grab on of their famous bakery items, get there in the morning quickly. You can easily locate the place by the queues outside the bakery, and we can assure you it is worth the hype! Their cinnamon roll was delightful, we the right amount of crispiness and a decadent amount of cinnamon sugar to keep us happy.

Rating: 10/10
#2 Kensington Bakery

London, United Kingdom

This place is easily on of our favorites. The danish is stunning, with summer fruits and a delicious custard. The bakery is slightly on the more expensive side, but we are talking about London here so this may be the norm. Highly recommend the bakery and would go there again in a heartbeat.

Rating: 9/10
#3 Xurreria

Barcelona, Spain

Can you say you have visited Barcelona without actually having churros every day you were there? We think not, which is why we tried a few places when we were there and Xurreria definitely came out on top! The churros were exceptionally crispy but what made all the difference was the chocolate sauce which wasn't too sweet and just perfect.

Rating: 8/10
